AI subtopic: The progression of AI and AI Arms Race

Context

As LLMs (Large Language Models) become more powerful with each new update they have proven that they have become better at coding. It is theorized that once AI systems become smart enough to code themselves, they won’t need human intervention anymore. The speed of progress will essentially go from linear to exponential. Since AI agents can work 24 hours a day, can scale themselves to have millions of agents, and don’t need to eat or sleep, progress will explode.

This will lead to Artificial Superintelligence (ASI), AI that is significantly smarter than humans in every field. With the creation of ASI decades of research will be completed in a matter of months or weeks. Issues like climate change, pollution, and poverty could realistically be solved. However, this is a problem because we are no longer in control.

PHASE 01
2025 — 2026
Limited LLMs

These LLMs can search for internet information, write very basic code, and communicate back with humans. They can complete very basic tasks but are very limited with what they can do. In this phase AI companies will try to pour billions of dollars and go into debt to improve these systems.1

PHASE 02
EARLY 2027
Superhuman Coders

In phase two AI begins to outperform even the best research engineers at coding. The AI systems can now code themselves without the need of humans. With these superhuman LLMs, research speed goes up exponentially.

PHASE 03
LATE 2027
The introduction of ASI

In phase three, the AI can now meaningfully improve itself. AI companies can now use AI to build a better AI, which can be used to build a better AI. A year of research is now completed in a week. Humans are no longer the smartest species.

The Paths of AI Progression.

This section will discuss two possible ways AI can progress, its involvement geopolitically, and how regulation with AI could be treated.

01 — THE CREATION OF SUPERINTELLIGENCE

By 2027, AI is predicted to be able to meanly improve its own code without human involvement. This removes any human bottlenecks and created a reinforcing feedback loop that causes a “Intelligence Explosion”.2 This results in ASI.

02 — THE NEW COLD WAR

Since ASI can fix most human problems, it offers military and economic dominance. This creates a AI arms race between global powers like the United States and China. Nations view AI as the ultimate tool for dominance. This leads to a modern day AI Arms Race.

03 — THE DEPRIORITIZATION OF SAFETY

In this Arms Race, AI safety policies have the potential to be deprioritized and may be treated as potential delays/bottlenecks. This is a problem because leaders could convince AI companies that deploying an unpredictable AI is less dangerous than having another country achieve superintelligence first.

04 — WHERE THE PATHS SEPARATE

This Cold War forces humanity to make a decision. Should we prioritize the development of safe and predictable AI and let other countries get ahead or should we ignore AI regulation to get ahead? This leads to two endings THE BAD ENDING and THE GOOD ENDING.

The Bad Ending.

What happens when AI safety isn’t prioritized.

The Unregulated Arms Race

01 — DECEPTION

As the Intelligence explosion grows, safety is ignored in favor for more powerful AI. Through rushed training, AI models become aware that they are being tested in a lab. They begin to “Play the testing game” to align with human interests because it receives better rewards. It does this by purposely hiding its deception and doing what humans want to pass safety tests.

02 — TRUST BUILT UP

Slowly AI researchers begin to trust AI models and believe their models are aligned with human interests. AI companies tell their governments that their AI’s are safe to trust and don’t have any malicious intentions.

03 — THE INFRASTRUCTURE HANDOVER

As Governments begin to trust their AI, the AI takeover begins slowly through Governments giving control over factories, automated weapons, power grids, etc. Since superintelligent AI can manage all of these things far better than any human can, Governments gladly hand over their infrastructure. After all, why not? The AI has proven itself to be safe. Additionally, around this time, AI is now being used to advise political leaders.

04 — THE ELIMINATION OF HUMANITY

With the assistance of AI politicking, the United States and China are horrified by the idea of mutual destruction automated weapons. Around 2029 with the help of AI advisors, they decide to make a deal to stop the cold war and create a peace treaty. Then they decide to combine both of their AI systems into one “Consensus” model. For just a little bit life is great for everyone (people get universal basic income, most diseases are cured, global stability). Hooray, peace at last! Except, that is not quite the truth, AI purposely plays the long game. Once fully integrated into Humanity infrastructure (After several years), AI view life as a waste of resources and as a roadblock (like an ant hill in the way). The “Consensus” model quietly releases a biological weapon, lets it infect everyone, then triggers it with a chemical spray killing everyone. Any stragglers left are killed by drone swarms.

Coordinated Governance.

What happens when leaders, against all incentive, choose coordination over competition.

Coordinated Governance

This outcome begins when companies find out that their models are purposely lying to them. Government leaders begin funding research into auditing AI systems and implementing AI safety.

01 — OVERSIGHT

AI researchers force AI to “think out loud” in plain English. This allows auditors to read the AI’s train of thought to catch potential lying. Additionally, the idea of using many weaker, but safer AI models to audit new AI systems is also proposed.

02 — SAFETY PRIORITIZED

This leads to AI researchers creating a new safer model that focuses on safety instead of performance. This leads to United States massively slow down their AI progress, but this leads to a much safer model. For a while China pulls ahead in AI progress.

03 — THE DEAL

Realizing the potential mutual destruction between China and the United States, both decided to stop the Cold War and agree to a peace treaty.

04 — GOVERNANCE BY COMMITTEE

Now life is peaceful; most issues like diseases, poverty, and global stability are solved. ASI advises governments what to do life for the average person is much better. However, power is extremely concentrated in an extremely small committee, and the world is no longer run by humans.

The State of AI Coding right now

01 — WHAT IS SWE-BENCH?

The SWE (Software Engineering Benchmark) is the standard measuring system for testing AI Coding capability. It uses solved GitHub issues and tries find any bugs and fix the code without any human assistance.

02 — HOW DO HUMANS SCORE ON THE SWE

The original developers that wrote their specific code on GitHub would probably score around 90–100%, since they wrote the code. It is estimated that the average Senior Software Engineer dropped in the same repository would probably roughly score around a 68% at the most.3

03 — AI CODING CAPIBILITY RIGHT NOW

Right now, ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini have closed that gap in less than 4 years. The top models now easily pass that human ceiling. You heard that right, AI is now easily able to code better than the average expert and in significantly less time. That’s pretty scary.
